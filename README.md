# 🏰 Warchester Citadel

The Citadel is the central platform of the Warchester ecosystem.

It combines the public treasury dashboard, citizen portal, and social layer into a single application where the growth of the city can be viewed and its activities accessed.

---

## Core Features

• Public treasury dashboard showing Warchester USDC reserves  
• Streaming revenue transparency  
• Citizen profiles and identity system  
• Entry point for Warchester games  
• Social layer for citizens of the city  

---

## Treasury Dashboard

The Citadel displays live treasury data including:

• USDC reserves  
• Streaming revenue growth  
• Ecosystem funding allocation  

This transparency allows citizens to track how the city grows over time.
